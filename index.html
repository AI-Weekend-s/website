What is AI Weekends?

AI Weekends is a vibrant community where AI enthusiasts, developers, and curious minds come together to learn, build, and share. Every session is packed with insights, hands-on learning, and the latest from the world of artificial intelligence.

Whether you're just starting your AI journey or you're a seasoned practitioner, there's something here for everyone. Join us and be part of the future!
